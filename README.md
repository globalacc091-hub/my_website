# ❄ Silver Skates KRG — Figure Skating School Website

A website for **Silver Skates** — the first amateur figure skating school in Karaganda, Kazakhstan.

## 🌐 About the Project

This is a single-page website built for a figure skating school. It includes information about the school, coach, schedule, pricing, and contact details.

**Built with:** HTML, CSS, Vanilla JavaScript — no frameworks, no dependencies.

## 📄 Pages & Sections

- **Hero** — main banner with call to action
- **About** — school history and key stats
- **Our Story** — timeline of the school's growth since 2018
- **Groups** — who we train (kids, adults, competitive)
- **Coach** — profile of head coach Yulia Samushchenko
- **Schedule** — weekly training timetable
- **Pricing** — subscription plans
- **Contacts** — address, phone, Instagram

## 🏫 About Silver Skates

Silver Skates was founded in 2018 as a small group of enthusiasts. Today it is the **#1 amateur figure skating school in Karaganda**, with multiple training groups and athletes competing across Kazakhstan, Moscow, and Saint Petersburg.

**Head Coach:** Yulia Samushchenko
- Master of Sport of the Republic of Kazakhstan
- Champion of Kazakhstan
- Graduate of Lesgaft National State University of Physical Education (St. Petersburg)
- 12 years in professional sport · 7 years of coaching
